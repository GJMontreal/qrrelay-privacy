QRRelay Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

QRRelay does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer.

Camera

The camera is used on-device to scan QR codes and barcodes. Camera data is never recorded or transmitted to the developer.

Location

If you enable the location parameter, your device's GPS coordinates are included in the request sent to your configured server. This data goes only to the server you configure — not to the developer.

Server Credentials

Bearer tokens, API keys, and Basic Auth credentials are stored in your device's Keychain. They are never transmitted to the developer.

Requests

Scanned codes are sent as GET requests to a server URL that you configure. The developer has no access to these requests or their contents.

Analytics & Tracking

None. No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.
